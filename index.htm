Step by Step explanation

Our applications feature algorithmically generated problems, so that you'll encounter all kinds of examples. In addition, we provide a sleek interface through which you can view and answer questions easily.

Engaging & Effective .

In addition to providing a wide variety of practice material, we provide the support to help you through it. Our explanations walk through the entire approach of solving a problem, so that you can understand the entire process behind solving problems.

Self Paced and Challenging.

All of us have our weaknesses. We understand. That's why we built statistics tracking into our applications, so you can identify where you need to improve on. There's no setup required, just start practicing and the app will take care of the rest.

Our Mission

We started OnGoLearn because we saw the way the current generation of kids get glued to video games, to divert their mind and to give something of educational value in their hand.

We are a purposeful team of software engineers, educators, and parents.  we hope to assist in the movement of bringing technology to the classroom and maintain an equilibrium. We are on a mission to change the way people learn.
