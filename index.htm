Aligned to common core standards.

We prepare our lessons based on common core standards. You can check out the review submitted by Larry Sullivan of Participate.com, which is a popular review site. Master Algebra satisfies lots of common core standards.

Step by Step explanation

Our applications feature different types of problems, for example multiple choice,fill in blanks, true false and many more so that you'll encounter all kinds of examples. In addition, we provide a easy interface through which you can view and answer questions easily.

Engaging & Effective .

In addition to providing a wide variety of practice material, we provide the support to help you through it. Our explanations walk through the entire approach of solving a problem, so that you can understand the entire process behind solving problems.

Self Paced and Challenging.

All of our apps are self paced. You can practice at your own time. You can carry on from the stage where you left last time. You can participate in challenge to compare with others, to see how good you are in comparison to others.

Our Mission

We started OnGoLearn because we saw the way the current generation of kids get glued to video games, to divert their mind and to give something of educational value in their hand.

We are a team of software engineers, educators, and parents.  we hope to assist in the movement of bringing technology to the classroom and maintain an equilibrium. We are on a mission to change the way people learn.
